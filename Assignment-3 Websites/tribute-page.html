Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, former President of India and the Missile Man
Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He was the chief architect of the Indian missile programs and played a pivotal role in the nation's nuclear tests. Revered as a visionary, Kalam inspired millions with his leadership, writings, and vision for a developed India.
